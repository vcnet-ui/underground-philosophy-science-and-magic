How this works

Attendees take a seat before a small stage in the underground vault beneath a cocktail bar. Throughout the evening, members of the audience and the Voicecraft Network will rotate places in dialogue on stage. Your spoken participation is optional. On booking your ticket, you can share a question, and indicate your interest to dialogue.
